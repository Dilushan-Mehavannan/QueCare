Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 5, 2026 • Last Updated: June 5, 2026 • Active Compliance Version

At QueueCare, we value your health and your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, protect, store, and utilize your personal information when you use the QueueCare Patient Mobile Application and the QueueCare Clinic Administration Dashboard.

Sections

1. Introduction 2. Information We Collect 3. How We Use Your Data 4. Location & Background Services 5. Security & Retention 6. Your Rights & Choice 7. Contact Information
01

Introduction

QueueCare operates a real-time queue management system intended to minimize waiting room congestion, optimize patient workflow, and help patients wait where they are most comfortable.

By installing the QueueCare mobile application or accessing our online dashboard, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We prioritize data protection compliance under standard health informatics privacy regulations and standard app store requirements.

02

Information We Collect

We collect information to deliver efficient real-time routing, ticket management, and wait-time notifications:

  • Account Registration Info: Your phone number, email address, name, and login credentials when registering.
  • Patient Profile Metadata: Optional information like age/date of birth and gender to display context for clinics.
  • Queue Interaction Records: History of booked slots, checked-in tickets, cancellations, no-show status, and live queue positions.
  • Device & Notification Identifiers: Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) tokens required to push live wait updates directly to your screen.

Note on Medical Information

QueueCare manages queue positions and appointments. We do not request, store, or process complete Electronic Medical Records (EMR) or diagnostic logs. Your tickets contain only basic queue classification tags (e.g., "General Checkup", "Pediatrics").

03

How We Use Your Data

We process data solely to execute the queue scheduling operations:

  • Queue Positioning: Generating and updating ticket sequences and status.
  • Wait-Time Predictions: Analyzing wait trends to estimate when you should arrive at the clinic.
  • Push Alerts & WebSocket Updates: Instantly notifying you when your queue number is near or when a delay occurs.
  • Clinic Communications: Sharing your checked-in status and name with the clinic administrative desk you are actively visiting.
04

Location Services & Background Tracking

To support clinic discovery and active ticket check-ins, the QueueCare mobile application utilizes location data:

1. Clinic Discovery

When you open the app to search for nearby medical services, we request access to your device location to list closest clinics.

2. Auto-Checkin / Geofencing

With your permission, we utilize geofencing to verify you have physically arrived at the clinic venue to update your ticket status to "Present".

Background Access: You may choose to deny background location access. In that case, you will simply have to check in manually via the app while it is open in the foreground, or request receptionist check-in. We do not sell or share location history logs with any third parties.

05

Data Security & Retention

All communications between the mobile app, dashboard portal, and server are secured using Transport Layer Security (TLS/HTTPS). JWT (JSON Web Tokens) are utilized to authenticate every request securely.

We retain account data as long as your account remains active. If your account remains inactive for over 24 months, or if you request deletion, we permanently purge or anonymize your logs, except as required to fulfill tax or legal recordkeeping.

06

Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction (such as under standard global data directives), you hold legal control over your personal records:

  • Access & Export: You can request copies of all records stored with QueueCare.
  • Correction: You can update your profile, phone number, and name directly inside the settings panel.
  • Erasure: You may submit a request to permanently terminate your account and wipe all transaction history.
  • Permission Toggles: You can turn off device location tracking, camera permissions (for QR codes), and push notifications at any point inside your OS system settings.
